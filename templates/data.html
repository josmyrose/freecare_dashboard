Data Categories

This dataset comprises detailed household and child health survey data collected from 1,652 observations across various treatment groups. It includes 132 variables spanning multiple dimensions such as:

  • Household Characteristics: Number of household members, income indicators, education, and occupation of the female adult.
  • Treatment Assignments: Participation in health interventions across four treatment arms.
  • Vaccination & Health Behavior: Vaccine records (BCG, Polio, DTP, Hepatitis B, Measles, etc.), illness episodes, healthcare-seeking behavior, and access to health workers.
  • Child Health Indicators: MUAC (Mid-Upper Arm Circumference), weight-for-age scores, breastfeeding duration, and nutritional status.
  • Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH): Water source, aquatabs use, water treatment behavior, and mosquito net usage.
  • Outcome Tracking: Weekly records of diarrhea episodes, blood/three-stool symptoms, and healthcare visits.
